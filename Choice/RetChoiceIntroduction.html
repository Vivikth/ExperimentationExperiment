In this stage of the experiment, you will choose some foods to eat for dinner.
Instead of selecting foods directly, you will have to choose a food-menu pair for your dinner.

What are food-menu pairs?

A food-menu pair consists of a single food, and a list of foods.
To complete a food-menu pair, you must first eat the single food.
After eating the single food, you will choose a food from the list and eat that food.

What you need to do

You will need to choose 2 food-menu pairs.
I will randomly select one food-menu pair for you to complete at dinner time.


Remember, the choices you make now will affect the food you eat at dinner!
