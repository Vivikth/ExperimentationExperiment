In this stage of the experiment, you will choose some foods to eat at the second session, and a sample to eat at this session.
Instead of selecting foods directly, you will have to choose a food-menu pair.

What are food-menu pairs?

A food-menu pair consists of a single food, and a list of foods.
To complete a food-menu pair, you must first eat the single food at the end of this session.
After eating the single food, you will be able to choose a food from the list at the second session and eat that food at the second session.

What you need to do

You will need to choose 2 food-menu pairs.
I will randomly select one food-menu pair for you to complete.
